These are some of the Frequently Asked Questions:

What is digital marketing?

Digital marketing is any kind of marketing that is geared towards smartphones, laptops, tablets and basically any other device that is capable of connecting to Wi-Fi.

Would my company benefit from digital marketing?

YES YES YES. You don’t have to replace your traditional marketing efforts with digital ones, but it is 2019 and if the word digital doesn’t come into your marketing strategy then your business will not thrive.

What is SEO?

Search Engine Optimisation is the practice of using on-page and off-page tweaks and tactics to ensure that your page ranks higher on search engines. An example of one of these tactics is making sure that this blog is a minimum of 1000 words because that’s what Google prefers. Read our blog, ‘What is On-Page SEO?’ to learn more!

Firstly, you should be focusing on the social media platforms with the most active users. These are (in order), Facebook, YouTube, Instagram, Twitter and LinkedIn. Next, you need to determine where your target audience spends their time and educate yourself on the demographic.
